Visit Kanyakumari

Experience the spectacular edge of India where three oceans meet. Watch breathtaking sunrises and sunsets from the exact same beach.

Top three activities to do at Kanyakumari

Visit the Thiruvalluvar Statue

Admire the iconic 133-foot stone statue of the Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar, standing majestically on a rocky island where three seas meet.

Take a Ferry to Vivekananda Rock

Enjoy a short boat ride to the famous memorial where Swami Vivekananda is believed to have meditated, offering peaceful surroundings and panoramic ocean views.

Watch Sunrise at Kanyakumari Beach

Experience one of India's most celebrated sunrises at the southern tip of the mainland, where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean converge.
